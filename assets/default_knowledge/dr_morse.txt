=== CORE PHILOSOPHY ===

Dr. Robert Morse teaches that the human body has two fundamental fluids: blood and lymph. While modern medicine focuses on blood, Dr. Morse focuses on the lymphatic system — the body's "sewer system." When the lymphatic system is stagnant, toxins and acids accumulate, causing all known diseases.

Key Principles:
- There are two sides to chemistry: acid and alkaline (base)
- Acids destroy tissues; alkalinity heals and regenerates
- The lymphatic system is the body's primary waste removal system
- The kidneys are the main exit door for lymphatic waste
- Fruits are the most powerful lymphatic movers and kidney filterers
- The adrenal glands control kidney filtration — weak adrenals = no filtration
- Herbs and fruits are the tools for regeneration; chemistry is the key

=== THE TWO FLUIDS ===

BLOOD (Arterial Side — Kitchen):
- Carries nutrition, oxygen, hormones to cells
- Feeds and nourishes the body
- pH: 7.35–7.45 (slightly alkaline)

LYMPH (Interstitial Side — Sewer):
- Carries cellular waste, metabolic acids, dead cells, chemicals
- Removes waste from around every cell
- When stagnant: inflammation, pain, tumors, skin conditions, fatigue
- Moved by: physical activity, deep breathing, rebounding, and FRUIT

=== ACIDOSIS ===

Acidosis is the root cause of ALL disease according to Dr. Morse:
- Cancer = extreme acidosis (cells mutate to survive acid environment)
- Diabetes = pancreatic weakness from acid damage
- Arthritis = acid deposits in joints
- Skin conditions = lymphatic acids being pushed out through skin (3rd kidney)
- Neurological issues = acids attacking nerve tissue

SOURCES OF ACIDOSIS:
- Proteins (especially animal): meat, dairy, eggs create sulfuric and phosphoric acids
- Grains (especially wheat, rice): create mucus and acid
- Processed foods, chemicals, pharmaceuticals
- Stress and negative emotions
- Lack of movement (lymph stagnation)

=== THE DETOX PROCESS ===

Dr. Morse's approach to healing:

1. ASSESSMENT: Check kidney filtration (cloudy urine = good), tongue coating, skin condition, energy levels
2. DIET CHANGE: Move toward fruits, berries, melons, grapes
3. HERBAL SUPPORT: Target weak organs with specific herbs
4. MOVEMENT: Walk, rebound (mini trampoline), deep breathing
5. EMOTIONAL HEALING: Release fear, anger, grief — emotions affect glands
6. PATIENCE: True regeneration takes time (months to years for chronic conditions)

=== HEALING CRISIS ===

Signs that detox is working:
- Skin eruptions (boils, rashes, acne) — acids leaving through skin
- Mucus discharge (nose, lungs, bowels)
- Cloudy, dark, or sediment-filled urine — kidneys filtering
- Temporary fatigue, headaches, body aches
- Emotional releases (crying, anger surfacing)
- These are POSITIVE signs — the body is cleaning house

=== KIDNEY FILTRATION ===

The most important marker of detox progress:
- Collect first morning urine in a clear glass jar
- Let it sit for a few hours
- Look for: cloudiness, sediment at the bottom, stringy material
- Clear urine = kidneys NOT filtering (bad)
- Cloudy urine with sediment = kidneys filtering lymph waste (good)
- Goal: consistent kidney filtration through fruit diet + herbs
